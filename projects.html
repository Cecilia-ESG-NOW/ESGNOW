Re-creation of Existing Materials

2024 · Montreal · Reclaimed textiles · Sustainable fashion

Re-created garment using post-consumer textile waste.

This project responds to the accumulation of non-degradable textile waste generated by extensive production models and fast fashion systems.

Instead of introducing new materials, the design process focused on re-creation and transformation of existing garments.

Sustainability is framed not as an abstract objective, but as a practice embedded in everyday material decisions.

Material Transformation as Daily Practice

2025 · Canada · Mixed media · Design research

Process documentation video.

This project explores how sustainability can be practiced through small-scale, repeated acts of material transformation.

The work positions design as a mediating practice between existing material realities and future-oriented responsibility.

Sustainability Beyond the Ideal

Conceptual project · Ongoing

This ongoing project examines the limits of sustainability when it is framed solely as an idealized future goal.

Through design research and critical reflection, the project argues for sustainability as an embedded, material, and temporal practice.
