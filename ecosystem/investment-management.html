Investment Management Principle

Investment management is not only about selecting assets. It is about understanding risk, return, time, valuation, market structure, and investor behavior.

A disciplined investor does not simply ask, “What should I buy?” A disciplined investor asks, “What risk am I taking, why am I compensated for it, and how does this position fit into a long-term portfolio system?”
