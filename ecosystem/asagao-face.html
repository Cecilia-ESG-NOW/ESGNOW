02 · Facial Ritual

ASAGAO WELLNESS · Face · Jaw · Sensory Softening

The Facial Ritual focuses on softening facial tension, releasing jaw tightness, calming sensory overload, and restoring a naturally relaxed expression through slow therapeutic touch.

Inspired by Eastern healing philosophy and quiet luxury wellness, the ritual supports emotional grounding, nervous system regulation, breathing rhythm, and deep restorative calm.
