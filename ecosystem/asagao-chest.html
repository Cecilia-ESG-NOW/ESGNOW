05 · Chest Release

ASAGAO WELLNESS · Chest · Breath · Openness

A gentle chest release ritual designed to support breath awareness, openness, emotional softening, and the release of upper-body tension through slow therapeutic touch.

Inspired by Eastern healing philosophy and quiet luxury wellness, Chest Release focuses on the chest as an important area for breath, posture, emotional holding, and nervous system regulation.
