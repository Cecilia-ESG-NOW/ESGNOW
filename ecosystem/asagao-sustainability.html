ASAGAO SUSTAINABILITY

ESG · Sustainability Systems · Green Finance · Ecological Research

ASAGAO Sustainability explores ecological design, reclaimed textiles, slow production, green finance, and sustainable fashion systems.

It is designed as a research space for material transformation, circular design, environmental responsibility, and long-term ecological aesthetics.

Featured Project

Material transformation · Slow production · Ecological aesthetics

Re-creation of Existing Materials

This project responds to the accumulation of non-degradable textile waste generated by extensive production models and fast fashion systems.

Instead of introducing new materials, the design process focused on re-creation and transformation of existing garments.

Sustainability is framed not as an abstract objective, but as a practice embedded in everyday material decisions.
