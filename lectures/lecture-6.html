The True Core of Traditional Chinese Medicine Wellness

The essence of TCM wellness is not about following an “absolute” schedule or rigid clock times.

Rather, it is about:

“Living in harmony with natural rhythms.”

  • Aligning with the cycle of day and night
  • Aligning with the changing seasons
  • Aligning with the body’s condition
  • Aligning with the different stages of age and life
  • Aligning with emotional rhythms and mental states

At its core, wellness is:

Reducing resistance against the body’s natural patterns and rhythms.

“Live in harmony with nature.”
— Traditional Chinese Medicine Philosophy
