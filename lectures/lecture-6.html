Lecture 6 · Cultural Studies of Traditional Chinese Medicine

Traditional Chinese Medicine · Wellness Culture · Daily Rhythms · EN

Introduction to Traditional Chinese Medicine wellness, daily rhythms, and holistic living principles.

Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) views human health as a dynamic balance between the body, mind, environment, and natural cycles.

Rather than focusing solely on disease treatment, TCM emphasizes prevention, regulation, and harmony with nature through daily lifestyle practices, emotional balance, sleep rhythms, nutrition, and seasonal adaptation.

The True Core of Traditional Chinese Medicine Wellness

The essence of TCM wellness is not about following an “absolute” schedule or rigid clock times.

Rather, it is about living in harmony with natural rhythms.

“Living in harmony with natural rhythms.”

  • Aligning with the cycle of day and night
  • Aligning with the changing seasons
  • Aligning with the body’s condition
  • Aligning with the different stages of age and life
  • Aligning with emotional rhythms and mental states

At its core, wellness is:

Reducing resistance against the body’s natural patterns and rhythms.
“Live in harmony with nature.”
— Traditional Chinese Medicine Philosophy
